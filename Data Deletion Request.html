How to Delete Your Data from "Watch Me Now"

  1. Submit a Request: To begin the data deletion process, please send an email to support@watchmenow.live with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."
  2. Provide Necessary Information: In your email, include your full name, email address associated with your account, and any other relevant information that will help us identify your account.
  3. Confirmation: We will send you a confirmation email acknowledging your request and provide an estimated timeline for completion.
  4. Completion: Once we have processed your request, we will send you a final confirmation email.

Please note that deleting your data will result in the loss of access to personalized features and may affect your ability to use certain aspects of "Watch Me Now."

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@watchmenow.live.
